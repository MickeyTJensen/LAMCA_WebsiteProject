Our Gym

Welcome to LAMCA Gym – where your health journey begins. More than just a fitness center, we at LAMCA Gym have cultivated a cohesive community where the passion for health and well-being is unwavering. Our name, composed of the initials of founders Lars, Anders, Mickey, Chung, and Arta, stands for diversity and expertise in fitness and personal training. Each day, we embrace our mission to highlight physical training as a cornerstone of a balanced lifestyle.

Gym Facilities

We offer a variety of fitness equipment, including top-notch treadmills and elliptical machines in our cardio section, all providing invigorating workouts with scenic beach views.

kettlebells

At Lamca Gym, kettlebells are key to reaching your fitness goals. Our expert trainers tailor personalized workouts for all fitness levels, ensuring maximum results and satisfaction.

weightlifting

Explore the strength-building benefits of weightlifting at our center, where our supportive environment and top-notch facilities help you achieve your fitness goals.

dumbbells

Elevate your strength training with our premium dumbbells and resistance bands at Lamca Gym. Join us today to start your journey to a stronger, healthier you!

Yoga Space

Step into our gym and immerse yourself in a sanctuary designed for holistic wellness. Here, alongside you'll also have the opportunity to explore the serene practice of yoga.

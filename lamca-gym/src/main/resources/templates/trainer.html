Trainers

"Certified Yoga and Pilates Wellness Coach"

Arta holds certifications in both Yoga and Pilates, offering a unique blend of these disciplines to enhance her clients' physical, mental, and emotional well-being. Her extensive training encompasses various yoga styles, including Hatha, Vinyasa, and Restorative Yoga, as well as a deep understanding of Pilates principles and techniques. This combination allows Arta to tailor her classes to the diverse needs of her clients, providing a comprehensive approach to health and wellness.

"Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist"

Mickey is a strength and conditioning coach with a focus on boosting individuals' strength and endurance through specific training regimens. Often working with athletes or individuals looking to improve their performance in a particular sport or activity, Mickey's programs include lifting techniques, plyometric exercises, and targeted conditioning workouts. His energetic and disciplined coaching style helps clients push beyond their limits.

"Certified General Fitness Instructor"

Lars is a general fitness trainer who specializes in helping clients achieve broad fitness goals such as improving overall physical condition, losing weight, gaining muscle mass, and enhancing general health. He designs personalized workout plans that incorporate a mix of strength training, cardio exercises, and flexibility workouts. With a friendly and motivational approach, Lars is passionate about helping individuals of all fitness levels make sustainable lifestyle changes.

"Certified Rehabilitation Fitness Trainer"

Anders is a rehabilitation trainer specializing in working with clients recovering from injuries or managing chronic conditions requiring customized exercise programs. With a background in physical therapy or rehabilitation, Anders collaborates closely with healthcare professionals to ensure a safe and effective recovery process. His empathetic and patient approach provides vital support to clients on their journey to recovery.

"Certified Holistic Fitness Trainer"

Chung is a Certified Weight Management and Functional Fitness Trainer with a specialized focus on helping clients achieve their weight loss goals and improve their overall functional abilities for daily activities. He designs personalized programs that blend strength training, cardiovascular exercises, and nutritional guidance to support weight management.

Trainers

Arta

"Certified Yoga and Pilates Wellness Coach"

Arta, certified in Yoga and Pilates, offers a unique approach to enhance clients' well-being. With expertise in various yoga styles and deep knowledge of Pilates principles, she tailors classes to diverse needs, ensuring a comprehensive approach to health and wellness.

Mickey

"Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist"

Mickey, a strength and conditioning coach, specializes in enhancing strength and endurance through targeted training. Working predominantly with athletes and those aiming to excel in their sports, his programs incorporate lifting techniques, plyometrics, and tailored conditioning workouts.

Lars

"Certified General Fitness Instructor"

Lars, a general fitness trainer, specializes in guiding clients toward diverse fitness objectives, including overall improvement, weight loss, muscle gain, and better health. He crafts personalized workout routines blending strength training and flexibility exercises.

Anders

"Certified Rehabilitation Fitness Trainer"

Anders, a rehabilitation trainer, specializes in aiding clients recovering from injuries or managing chronic conditions with personalized exercise programs. With a background in physical therapy, he collaborates closely with healthcare professionals for a safe recovery.

Chung

"Certified Holistic Fitness Trainer"

Chung, a Certified Weight Management and Functional Fitness Trainer, specializes in assisting clients with weight loss goals and enhancing functional abilities for daily tasks.He tailors programs combining strength training, and nutrition for effective weight management.
